About

This website is about CASS Social Enterpirse. CASS is dedicated to building a "Community-based Kitchen System‚Äù in China. It is a social enterprise established in 2017 to solve the employment problem of disabled and enable them to start their own business. CASS runs with three product lines: 1. Group/business meals; 2. Fine dining restaurant; 3. Stewed meats and vegetables, which interprets the core competitiveness and spreads love through food transmission. At present, there are 35 disabled employees and 30 deaf employees. CASS expects to employ 1000 disabled workers and enable 100 disabled people to start their own business while providing 500,000 elder meals in five years.

Objective

The objective of this website is to introduce CASS Social Enterprise, and more importantly to spread the voice of CASS.

Target Audience

The target audience is the fans of social enterprise and b-corps. While this website also aims to show CASS Social Enterprise in front of charitable foundations, banks, corporations, and impact investments to seek for more fundings.
